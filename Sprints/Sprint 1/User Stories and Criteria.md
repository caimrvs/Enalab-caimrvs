# Users Stories 

### Definition

The users stories are part of agile development organization, is part Epic, Funcionality, User Stories and Tasks diagram. 
Epic say about significative product submit, on the business vision. Funcionality says about little product submit. User stories are littles actions of users with product.

# DoD, DoR and Acceptance criteria

### Definition

The definition of done, definition of ready, and acceptance criteria are checklists used to determine if a task is ready to be performed or completed. The avaliations criteria are in the DoD and DoR to assess the criteria necessary for the completion of User Stories.
