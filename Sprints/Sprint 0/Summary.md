On this day, I studied the Scrum methodology and Kanban Board.

The Scrum is one methodology on that make a easy project organization. Organized on the "Sprints" process, passed on 1 to 4 weeks. The Kanban Board is one Board on that organized the tasks of group or person. The tasks stay organized in the three columns. The columns is: To do, In progress and Done.
